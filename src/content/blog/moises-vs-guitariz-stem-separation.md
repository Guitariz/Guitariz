AI-driven **audio source separation** has fundamentally transformed how producers remix tracks, how singers practice acapellas, and how instrumentalists dissect complex song arrangements. 

By analyzing mixed stereo audio files (MP3, WAV, FLAC), modern neural networks can split songs into distinct isolated channels—separating vocals, drums, basslines, guitars, and keyboards.

Two of the most widely discussed platforms for online stem separation in 2026 are **Moises.ai** and **Guitariz Studio**. In this breakdown, we compare both tools on isolation quality, speed, pricing, and limitations.

---

## 1. Moises.ai Overview

**Moises** is one of the pioneer commercial applications for AI audio separation. Available across iOS, Android, and web browsers, Moises offers a suite of tools including pitch shifting, tempo change, chord detection, and stem separation.

### Moises Strengths:
* Smooth mobile app user interface.
* Smart metronome with count-in generation.
* Lyric sync for karaoke practice.

### Moises Limitations (Free Tier):
* **5 Songs / Month Limit:** Free accounts can only separate up to 5 tracks per month.
* **5-Minute Audio Cap:** Uploaded audio tracks longer than 5 minutes are truncated.
* **Standard Resolution:** High-fidelity separation models (`Hi-Fi`) are restricted to paid subscribers ($3.99/mo or $39.99/yr).
* **Mandatory Account Registration:** Requires signing up with an email address before processing any audio.

---

## 2. Guitariz Studio Overview

[Guitariz Studio](https://guitariz.studio/stem-separator) is a free, web-based open-source digital workbench powered by **Meta Research's Demucs v4** deep learning architecture.

### Guitariz Strengths:
* **100% Free & Unlimited:** No monthly track limits, no account registration, and no song duration caps.
* **State-of-the-Art Demucs Engine:** Employs Meta's hybrid transformer model to split audio into up to 6 clean stems (**Vocals**, **Drums**, **Bass**, **Guitar**, **Piano**, and **Other**).
* **Dedicated Micro-Tools:** Instant 1-click access to dedicated single-purpose tools like the [Vocal Remover](https://guitariz.studio/vocal-remover), [Key Finder](https://guitariz.studio/key-detector), and [BPM Detector](https://guitariz.studio/bpm-detector).
* **Full DAW Export:** Download isolated stems as uncompressed 24-bit WAV files or high-bitrate MP3s directly into your DAW.
* **Integrated Theory Lab:** View the detected musical key, tempo, and synchronized chord progressions alongside your separated stems.

## Performance & Audio Quality Comparison

When extracting vocals or isolating basslines, **bleed** (artifacts where drums or guitars leak into the vocal channel) is the main indicator of AI quality.

* **Acapella Extraction:** Both platforms produce exceptionally clean vocal tracks on modern studio recordings. On dense rock or heavy metal tracks with distorted guitars, **Guitariz's Demucs engine** maintains clearer vocal transients with fewer phase artifacts.
* **Bass & Low-End Separation:** Guitariz isolates sub-bass frequencies and bass guitar transients with crisp definition, making it ideal for bass transcribers and hip-hop sample producers.
