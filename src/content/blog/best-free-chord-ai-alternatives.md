If you are looking to transcribe your favorite songs, **Chord AI** is one of the most popular mobile tools available. However, because it requires installing an app and locks premium chords (like 7ths, diminished, and slash chords) behind subscription plans, many musicians look for a free online alternative.

In this guide, we will compare the top web-based chord detectors and explain how you can extract chords from MP3s and YouTube videos completely for free.

---

## 1. Guitariz Chord AI (Web-Based & 100% Free)

If you want the power of Chord AI without downloading any apps or typing in credit card details, [Guitariz Chord AI](https://guitariz.studio/chord-ai) is the best online alternative.

### Why it stands out:
* **Stateless and Private:** Your files are processed entirely in memory and are deleted immediately after analysis. Your data is 100% private.
* **Interactive Chord Diagrams:** View real-time guitar fingerings and diagrams synced with the playback of your song.
* **Transposition Controls:** Pitch-shift the audio and transpose chords instantly to match your preferred key or vocal range.
* **Detailed Confidence Scores:** View an analytical graph displaying how confident the neural network is on each chord detection.

---

## 2. Chordify

Chordify is a well-known chord transcription website. It has a large catalog of pre-analyzed YouTube tracks and allows you to upload your own files.

### The Catch:
While the basic version is free, it restricts key features behind a monthly premium paywall. In the free version, you cannot transpose songs, edit wrong chords, or download MIDI files, and the interface is heavily cluttered with banner ads.

---

## 3. AnthemScore (Desktop Software)

AnthemScore is professional-grade desktop software that uses machine learning to transcribe audio files into full sheet music and MIDI.

### The Catch:
Unlike browser-based tools, AnthemScore requires downloading and installing software on a PC or Mac. It is also not free—it offers a 30-day trial, after which you must purchase a lifetime license starting at $29.

---

## Which one should you choose?

If you are looking for professional, sheet-music-grade transcribing for composition, a desktop tool like **AnthemScore** is powerful. However, if you are a guitarist or keyboardist looking to quickly play along with an MP3, WAV, or YouTube video, a free online app like [Guitariz Chord AI](https://guitariz.studio/chord-ai) gives you premium features with absolutely no friction.
