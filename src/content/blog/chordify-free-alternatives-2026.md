When learning new songs on guitar, piano, or ukulele, **Chordify** has long been one of the most recognizable web platforms for automatic chord recognition. By searching YouTube links or uploading audio files, musicians get an automated animated chord sheet synced to playback.

However, over recent years, **Chordify's free tier has become increasingly restrictive**. Key essential tools—such as transposing audio, pitch shifting, downloading MIDI files, editing chord voicings, and isolating vocals—are locked behind a monthly paid subscription (`Chordify Premium`). Furthermore, free users are often bombarded with intrusive video and banner ads.

If you are looking for a powerful, web-based alternative that gives you premium chord detection features without subscription paywalls, **Guitariz Studio** is the leading free alternative in 2026.

## What to Look for in a Free Chordify Alternative

Before comparing platforms, here are the non-negotiable features modern musicians need from an AI chord transcribing tool:

1. **Unrestricted Chord Complexity:** Full support for 7th chords, minor 7ths, diminished, augmented, and slash chords (e.g., `C/E`, `G/B`).
2. **Instant Key Transposition:** Pitch-shift audio up or down in real time to match your capo or vocal range.
3. **MIDI Export:** Instant 1-click export of detected harmonic progressions to `.mid` files for DAW import.
4. **Vocal & Instrumental Stem Filtering:** AI-driven source separation to remove vocals for cleaner chord extraction on dense mixes.
5. **No Registration or Paywall:** Instant browser analysis without forced account creation.

## 2. Moises AI

**Moises** is a popular mobile and web platform primarily focused on AI stem separation and speed/pitch adjustment. It also includes an automatic chord detection feature.

### The Catch:
While Moises excels at stem isolation, its free tier restricts users to **5 audio separations per month** and caps uploaded audio tracks at 5 minutes in length. Advanced MIDI export and high-fidelity Demucs models require a paid subscription.

---

## 3. Yamaha Chord Tracker (Mobile App Only)

Yamaha offers a dedicated mobile app (`Chord Tracker`) that automatically analyzes audio stored locally on your iOS or Android device.

### The Catch:
Chord Tracker is strictly a mobile app—it lacks a web version, cannot analyze YouTube audio directly, and does not support MIDI export or multi-instrument stem separation.
